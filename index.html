Patient Attendance Insights Dashboard

This project gave me a chance to dig into real-world healthcare data and uncover what really drives patient attendance. Using Excel, I built a clean, interactive dashboard that breaks down appointment trends by age group, appointment type, and SMS reminders. The goal was simple — to help healthcare teams understand how communication and scheduling habits impact patient turnout. Through this analysis, I identified patterns that could guide outreach strategies and reduce no-shows overall.

    Emergency Room Performance

    This project analyzes emergency room data to understand when hospitals experience their highest patient volumes. Using Tableau, I visualized patterns in patient flow, wait times, and satisfaction to uncover insights that help optimize staffing and improve the overall patient experience.
